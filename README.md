# Eternal_Limited_DCF_Model

# About This Project
This is an independent equity research and financial modelling project on Eternal Limited (NSE/BSE: ETERNAL) - India's largest consumer commerce platform operating across Food Delivery, Quick Commerce (Blinkit), Going-Out (District), and B2B Supplies (Hyperpure).
The model is built entirely from scratch using publicly available annual reports, BSE/NSE filings, and earnings call transcripts. It covers FY22A–FY25A historical data and projects financials through FY29E

# Model Structure
The workbook is organized into the following sheets:
Cover - Company overview, stock information, key metrics snapshot and navigation guide
Historical Financials - Full P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement for FY22A–FY25A
KPIs - Group-level and segment-wise operating KPIs
Projections - Revenue, EBITDA, and PAT forecasts for FY26E–FY29E
DCF Valuation - WACC build, free cash flow projection, terminal value, and sensitivity analysis
SOTP Valuation - Segment-wise valuation build across Food, Blinkit, Hyperpure, and District
Comps Analysis - EV/Revenue, EV/EBITDA, and P/E peer benchmarking
Assumptions - Key driver assumptions and scenario toggles

# Financial Highlights
Revenue grew from ₹4,193 Cr in FY22 to ₹20,243 Cr in FY25 - a 3-year CAGR of ~70%, driven by Blinkit's rapid scale-up and Hyperpure's expansion. Revenue is projected to reach ~₹65,160 Cr by FY29E.
Adj. EBITDA turned positive in FY24 at ₹372 Cr after deep losses in FY22 (₹-973 Cr) and FY23 (₹-765 Cr). Margin is expected to expand from 5.3% in FY25 to ~14.4% by FY29E as operating leverage kicks in.
PAT profitability was achieved in FY24 at ₹351 Cr, a major milestone after consecutive loss years. PAT is projected to grow to ₹6,039 Cr by FY29E.
Blinkit EBITDA breakeven was achieved in Q3FY26 - a critical inflection point in the investment thesis.

# Segments at a Glance
🍔 Food Delivery — The original and most mature business. Generates revenue through platform commissions and delivery fees. Contributed ₹8,080 Cr in FY25 and remains the primary profitability engine with EBITDA of ₹1,505 Cr.
⚡ Blinkit (Quick Commerce) — 10-minute grocery delivery via dark store network. Fastest growing segment at ₹5,206 Cr in FY25. Targeting 4,500 stores by FY29E.
🏪 Hyperpure (B2B Supplies) — Restaurant-grade ingredient and supply chain business. Revenue of ₹6,196 Cr in FY25, growing at ~38% CAGR through FY29E.
🎭 District / Going-Out — Events, dining reservations, and ticketing. Early stage at ₹737 Cr in FY25 but a long-term optionality play.
